# Code of conduct

Solar contributors and community members must keep project spaces welcoming,
technical, and respectful.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate of different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Critique code and ideas, not people.
- Ask for clarification before assuming bad intent.
- Keep reports focused, reproducible, and free of private data.
- Respect maintainer decisions about scope, safety, and release readiness.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized content, deliberate disruption,
doxing, publication of private information, and sustained personal attacks are
not accepted in issues, discussions, pull requests, or other Solar spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove contributions and may temporarily
or permanently restrict participation when behavior harms the community. For a
private conduct report, use the repository's private security contact and mark
the report as a community conduct matter. Do not disclose sensitive details in
public.
